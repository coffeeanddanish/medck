definition 
hct over 65% in term infants 

causes 
increased erythropoesis from intrauterine hypoxia: 
maternal diabetes 
hypertension 
smoking; 
IUGR 

erythycyte transfusion: 
delayed cord clamping
twin-twin transfusion 

genetic or metabolic 
hypo/hyperthyroidism 
genetic trisomy of 13, 18, 21 

clinical presentations 
asympotmatic (most common)
ruddy skin 
hypoglycemia, hyperbilirubinemia 
respiratory distress, cyanosis, apnea 
irritability, jitteriness 
abdominal distension 

treatment 
IV fluids 
glucose 
partial exchange transfusion
